About us

Since 2003, our group of companies have been striving to provide best possible services in upstream in the Caspian region. Throughout the years of our successful operation, we have expanded a wide range of our products, signed and went through with several M&A deals with companies-major market players, and have transformed into an international holding. Today ASAA GROUP is a technological and multicultural company in currently emerging markets of Kazakhstan and Russia that is oriented to both Singapore and USA markets. Our current business drivers are Engineering, IT, Construction in various sectors of economics such as energy, infrastructure and finance. All of these activities are primarily focused on sustainable development making a good use of new advanced technological solutions in every step of our business ...We are a big, cohesive, competitive advantageous, dedicated to bringing energy, ideas and pride to their work team of the best experts in various fields sharing common goals and the way to success. We are bound by common business values which drive us to developing and improving our services continuously. We are in constant search for the best ideas of science and disciplines, which may improve human life, reduce labour costs in industry and boost labour force. Our main principle lies in developing technologies, which will allow for replacement of human labour force with software and robots to achieve the maximal efficiency with less cost. Thus, our company can be recognized as developing with a clear outlook and focus on growth. We are open and ready to cooperation anywhere in the world, with any company sharing the same objectives and basic goals. High level of qualification and professionalism of our experts is very important to us since we aim at rendering quality services. Therefore, we extensively invest in training local staff in countries, where our projects are implemented.
