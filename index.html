About us

Since 2003, our group of companies have been striving to provide best possible services in upstream in the Caspian region. Throughout the years of our successful operation, we have expanded a wide range of our products, signed and went through with several M&A deals with companies-major market players, and have transformed into an international holding. Today ASAA GROUP is a technological and multicultural company in currently emerging markets of Kazakhstan and Russia that is oriented to both Singapore and USA markets. Our current business drivers are Engineering, IT, Construction in various sectors of economics such as energy, infrastructure and finance. All of these activities are primarily focused on sustainable development making a good use of new advanced technological solutions in every step of our business.
